Step into the Dark History of the Nazgûl

The Nine Ringwraiths, once mighty kings, are now bound to Sauron’s will, transformed into spectral beings by the corruption of their Rings. Shadows of the Nine uncovers their origins, revealing how these men succumbed to the lure of power and became the most feared servants of the Dark Lord.

The Witch-king of Angmar

The Witch-king, once a mortal king, became the leader of the Nazgûl after succumbing to the power of one of the Nine Rings. He ruled over Angmar with fear and dark magic, relentlessly attacking the northern kingdom of Arnor. His legacy of terror was sealed during the Siege of Minas Tirith.

Khamûl: The Shadow of the East

Khamûl, the second most powerful of the Nazgûl, was an Easterling chieftain who became Sauron’s most loyal servant. Known for his command over Dol Guldur, he relentlessly hunted for the One Ring in the North. Though weakened by daylight, his terror and cunning made him a formidable force.
